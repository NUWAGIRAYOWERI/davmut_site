About Us

Davmut ku-Bendecido is a religious based organisation, that was established to transform lives, empowering marginalised communities through suitable development Education, Innovation and Religious Advocacy. Davmut is invested in education, creativity and innovation, spiritual growth through worshipping as one of the pro-found ways to get closer to God Our main focus is on promoting & protecting the interests and beliefs of praising and worshiping God. Hence why we have davmut worship city, a 24 hour interceding place available for booking by churches and individuals to spend time away with God.

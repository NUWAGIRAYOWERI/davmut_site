Objectives

Work with Student Service providers to develop and implement Career and Employability strategies.
Lead the Wok Placement initiatives.
Ensure institutions are securing high quality work experience and work placement opportunities for students
Develop strong partnerships with a wide range of stakeholders in the communities

Objectives

Lead on the development and delivery of a range of campaign material and activities.
Develop projects using a coaching style and provision of expert advice and appropriate measures for improvement.
Working with universities, colleges, schools and other educational settings, supporting efforts to increase access to affordable and higher quality education
Supporting innovation and creativity, through partnerships and inter- and transdisciplinary approaches and strengthening the role of higher education regionally.

Objectives

Developing business partnerships aimed at promoting work-based learning in all its forms
Promoting entrepreneurship education and social entrepreneurship among Graduates and young people.
Introducing opportunities for, the initial and continuous professional development of practical skills trainers and mentors in both universities and work-based settings.
Enhancing access to training and qualifications for all unemployed, through establishing feedback tracking and collection systems to adapt provision based on outcomes.

EDUCATION

Innovating to overcome barriers to access to learning.We believe that everyone has a right to basic education.However, in many parts of the world children, particularly in developing countries are denied this right. Therefore we have decided to open up local schools to create opportunities for both young people to join education as well as graduates to work in these schools to gain practical experience that enables them to implement their core skills. Additionally we work on Strengthening capacities of teachers & encouraging personal centered learning. We help educational settings, identify developmental needs and determine the best strategy of delivering the best student recruitment and retention plan and assist on the implementation process.
